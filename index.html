I'm writing this to make a point. You'll see. However I'm desperately afraid that I won't find the right words to make that point. You'll see. I'm afraid that all of the acrobatics of the medium, all the posturing and pomp will be lost in the vacuousness of the words themselves. I'm also slightly nervous that everyone will see through this rather thin attempt to rip off Austin Kleon. He is the one who said "steal like an artist" after all and I can't help but wonder how many people went right to his own jugular after that comment and ripped him off wholesale.
